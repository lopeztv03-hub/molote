Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 14, 2025

Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how the TikTok Bot ("Service") handles information. The Service is a personal utility used for testing and demo purposes. It does not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

Data Collection

  • No personal data collection: The Service does not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or identifiers.
  • Local-only operation: Any configuration values (such as API keys) are stored locally on your device using an .env file.
  • No telemetry: The Service does not send analytics or usage data to external servers.

Third-Party Services

If you enable real TikTok API access, authentication is performed via TikTok's OAuth flow. Tokens are stored locally and are under your control. The Service does not transmit these tokens to any third party.

Security

Keep API credentials secure on your device. Do not share your .env file. If a credential is compromised, revoke it via the provider dashboard and generate new ones.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date above.

Contact

For questions, contact the app owner via the profile or email associated with the distribution channel.
